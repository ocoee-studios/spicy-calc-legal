Privacy Policy for SpicyCalc

Last Updated: June 8, 2026

Ocoee Studios ("we," "us," or "our") operates the SpicyCalc mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with your use of the App.

Summary: SpicyCalc stores all of your data locally on your own device. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or have access to your personal information.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

SpicyCalc does not require an account, login, or registration. We do not collect, store on our servers, or transmit any personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, phone number, or location.

We do not use advertising networks, behavioral-analytics, or cross-app tracking technologies within the App. (Our purchase provider, RevenueCat, processes purchase data for subscription analytics as described in Section 5.)

2. Information Stored Locally on Your Device

The App allows you to enter and save information for your own personal use, including:

  • Calculator inputs and saved peptide configurations
  • Body metrics (weight, body measurements, height, age, sex)
  • Water intake logs
  • Food and nutrition logs
  • Fitness activity logs
  • Injection/shot calendar entries
  • Personal notebook entries
  • App preferences (theme, language, units)

All of this information is stored only on your device using your device's local storage. It never leaves your device, is never transmitted to us or any third party, and we have no ability to access it. If you delete the App, this data is removed from your device.

3. Optional iCloud Backup

The App may offer the ability to export your data as a file (for example, a JSON backup) so you can save it yourself or restore it later. If you choose to store this file in Apple iCloud or another service of your choosing, that data is handled under the terms of that service. We do not receive or have access to these backups.

4. Apple Health / Health Data Export

If the App offers the ability to export data (such as a CSV file of your logs), this file is created on your device and shared only where you direct it. We do not receive this data.

5. In-App Purchases

The App offers a one-time in-app purchase processed by Apple through the App Store and managed via RevenueCat, a third-party purchase-management service. Apple handles your payment; we never receive or store your payment card details. RevenueCat processes a pseudonymous app-user identifier and your purchase/entitlement status so the App can unlock the purchase and restore it, and may use this purchase data for its own analytics (such as aggregate revenue reporting). Apple's and RevenueCat's privacy policies apply to that data. We may also receive anonymized, aggregated sales reports from Apple that do not identify individual users.

6. Optional News Feature

If the App offers an optional news feature that retrieves articles from third-party sources, and you choose to enable it (for example, by providing your own third-party API key), requests may be made to those third-party services. Any API key you enter is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to us. Your use of third-party feeds is subject to those providers' privacy practices.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for adults aged 18 and older and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

8. Data Security

Because your data is stored locally on your device and not transmitted to us, the security of that data depends on the security of your own device. We recommend using your device's built-in security features (passcode, biometric lock, encryption).

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted at this location with an updated "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
