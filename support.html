Purchase and Restore Help

SpicyCalc uses a one-time, non-consumable Apple In-App Purchase for lifetime access. There is no subscription or recurring charge.

To restore a previous purchase, open the SpicyCalc purchase screen and tap Restore Purchase while signed in with the Apple ID that originally completed the purchase.

Local Data and Backups

Personal entries are stored locally on the device. Use the in-app export feature before deleting the App or moving to another device if you want to preserve local records.

Reporting a Problem

When contacting support, include the SpicyCalc version, iPhone model, iOS version, the screen where the issue occurred, and a description of what happened. Do not email private health or personal information unless it is necessary to explain the issue.

Important Notice
